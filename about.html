ON-GRID

An on-grid solar system is directly connected to the local power grid. It allows you to use solar power during the day and send excess energy back to the grid, earning credits through net metering. Key benefits include:

  • Cost Savings: Reduced electricity bills with payback through energy credits.
  • Ease of Installation: Ideal for homes, businesses, and industrial spaces.
  • No Battery Dependency: Eliminates the need for costly battery storage.

OFF-GRID

An off-grid solar system operates independently of the power grid. It uses batteries to store energy, making it a reliable solution for remote or rural areas. Perfect for those seeking complete energy independence.

  • Best For:-

    • Remote homes, farms, or businesses where grid extension costly.Locations with frequent power outages.Eco-conscious individuals or organizations seeking energy independence.system operates independently of the power grid.
